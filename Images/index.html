About Vasavi Staram

Serving Devotees with Love and Devotion

We are a unique organization called Vasavi Staram established to provide excellent facilities for pilgrims visiting sacred places. Our organization came into being with the vision of offering free meals and comfortable stay facilities in an ambient atmosphere.

Vasavi Staram has been providing peerless services to devotees at nominal maintenance charges, winning laurels from different corners. We operate branches at prominent pilgrim places providing accommodation, annadhanam (free meals), and various other services.

Our commitment extends beyond just providing shelter - we also support education through our Vidyadhanam program, offering financial assistance to economically backward and meritorious students.
